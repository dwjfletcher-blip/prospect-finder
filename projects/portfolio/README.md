# David Fletcher — Developer Portfolio

A clean, professional portfolio site that positions David as a capable web developer and converts visitors into conversations.

**Status:** Planning

**Key Date:** Build before Lead Scout outreach begins

---

## Problem It Solves

Without a portfolio, David has nothing to show when approaching clients. This is the foundation every other sales effort depends on — build this first.

## Target Client

Small business owners in the Philly suburbs who need a website or web app.

---

## In Scope

- Hero section with clear value proposition ("I build fast, professional websites for local businesses")
- Projects section (start with 1 project — personal dev site — add more as built)
- Short about section (MSP background = credibility with business owners)
- Contact section with simple form or direct email CTA
- Mobile responsive

## Out of Scope

- Blog
- Animations beyond subtle hover states
- CMS or dynamic content
- Any paid services or hosting

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML + Tailwind
- **Hosting:** Vercel (free, clean URL to send to prospects)

## Estimated Timeline

3-5 days.

## Success Criteria

David can send a URL to a prospective client and it clearly communicates what he builds, who he's built for, and how to get in touch.
